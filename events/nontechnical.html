Angry Bird

DESCRIPTION

Angry bird is the most popular computer game now a days. But it can be implemented practically by arranging various blocks of different shapes and sizes. As we know that in angry birds the blocks are hit by using different birds. In this game a large catapult shall be used to accelerate a ball to hit the target. Hitting of target helps to gain points.

SPECIFICATIONS


Individual Participation

Game Settings
• Each participant will be given 1 minute to configure their settings.

RULES AND REGULATIONS


Round 1:-
• There will be 5 boxes in one column.
• Point for the boxes will be 10,20,30 and 40 respectively.
• Point for the jackpot box is 60.
• There will be total 5 columns in 1st round.
• Total points for offer in 1st round is 600.
• To qualify 1st round minimum 250 points are required.
• 2 min. For each candidate.
• Every candidate will get 3 balls, 1 ball at a time.

Round 2:-
• There will be 4 boxes in each column of points 10,20,30,40 respectively.
• There will be hard boxes between the above boxes which will have zero points.
• Points for the jackpot box is 60.
• There will be 4 columns.
• Total points for offer in 2nd round is 450.
• To qualify 2nd round minimum 200 points are required.
• 1.30 min. for each candidate.
• Every candidate will get 2 balls and 1 tennis ball, 1 ball at a time.

Round 3:-
• In the final round ball has to pass through the ring, it is mandatory.
• There will be 4 boxes in each column of points 10,20,30 & 40 respectively.
• There will be hard boxes between the above boxes which will have zero points.
• Jackpot box will be of 60 points.
• Participants going maximum points will be winner.
• 1 min. For each candidate.
• Every candidate will get 1 ball & 2 tennis balls. 2 tennis ball at a time.

Note- Common rule for all level is that if boxes are drown on the table will not be counted.

REGISTRATION FEES


Rs.50/- Per Participant

JUDGEMENT CRITERIA


• If the ball falls after taking it into the ground it will not be calculated.
• If there occurs a tie between two participants and then golden chance will be given.
• If all the participants can’t cross the minimum criteria of the points then the maximum points will be decided according to the participants point.
• The above mentioned points will be same for all the rounds.

Nirmaan

INTRODUCTION

Ever felt like trying your hand at structure designing? Ever wondered how it would feel if you could construct something that could make a difference? This event at “Laqshya-2015” tests the mettle of student to come up with models with an innovative and path-breaking idea with attractive prize money at stake. Design a resource and time optimized structure that satisfies the constraints mentioned in the problem.

SPECIFICATIONS

Team – Maximum 2 participants per team

RULES & REGULATION


• The event will be held in three rounds.
• The final testing of the structures will be done on the same day of completion of event.

ROUND 1 - To make a model of ice cream sticks as per given structure.
• To make a model of given structure as shown in the model/snaps.
• A model is to be made from ice cream sticks and fevicol.
• The number of sticks provided will be limited and no extra sticks will be given.
• The model should be made in limited time.
• If two models are made in same time then judgement will be based upon the accuracy of the model.

ROUND 2: To make a model from match sticks/toothpicks as per the given structure.
• Teams will be given different structure via chit system.
• Teams need to make models according to given structure made of match sticks/toothpicks.
• Materials (matchsticks, toothpicks, thermacols, tissue paper, fevicol and thread) will be provided.
• The models have to be made in limited time.
• If two models are made in same time then judgement will be based upon the accuracy of the model.

ROUND 3: To make a model from ice-cream sticks/match sticks/toothpicks as per the given structure.
• Teams need to make the chosen structure using the given materials.
• Materials such as ice cream sticks, toothpicks, match sticks, thermacols, fevicol, tissue paper, thread will be provided.
• Limited time will be allotted to the individual teams.
• Judgment will be based on the accuracy of the structure.

Rs. 100/- per team

Su-du-ko

INTRODUCTION

If you are fond of playing puzzles, solving math problems, and think logic reasoning you can actually combine them all in solving a Sudoku puzzle. Sudoku, commonly known as Number Place, is a logic placement puzzle. The aim of the puzzle is to place the numbers from 1 to 9 in each cell of a 9x9 grid that is made up of 3x3 sub grids or regions. To start playing the game, you are given numbers in some cells referred to as “givens”. The goal of the puzzle is to fill in all of the blank cells with the numbers 1-9, with each number appearing only once in sub grid, rows and column.

SPECIFICATIONS


Individual participation

RULES & REGULATIONS

• To solve a given puzzle in specified time.
• There will be three rounds in this event.
• Round 1: Preliminary level (time duration -10 min)
• Round 2 : Middle level (time duration -15 min)
• Round 3 : Advanced level (time duration -20 min)
• Each box contains 1 mark.
• Selection should be based on the correct digit filling.
• There will be no negative marking.
• Result will be declared after each batch.
• Participants can re-register only up to first round of the event.
• Participation certificate will be provided to all the participants.

Rs.50/- per participant
