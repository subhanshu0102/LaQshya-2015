Counter Strike 1.6

INTRODUCTION

"If we ever hope to get rid of the terrorism from the world, the rule must be clear: One does not deal with terrorists; one does not bargain with terrorists; one killterrorists. "Counter-Strike is the #1 first person shooter (FPS) game in the world. Counter-Strike pits a team of counter-terrorists against a team of terrorists in a series of rounds. Each round is won by either completing the mission objective or eliminating the opposing force.

SPECIFICATIONS

Team:-
• Maximum 5 members per team.

Game Version:-
• Counter Strike 1.6 Protocol 48

Available Maps:- • de_dust2
• de_inferno
• de_nuke
• aim_aztec

RULES & REGULATIONS


Game Setting
• Teams will be given 3 minutes to configure their settings and after that use of console or changing the settings will be strictly prohibited.
• No .cfg files are allowed.
General Rules • The tournament organizers reserves the right to cancel or modify the tournament rules if fraud, technical failure or any other factor beyond reasonable control which can impair the integrity of the tournament.
• Numbers of Levels are dependent on number of participants.
• The tournament will follow the knock out criterion . A team winning the stage will advance to the next stage. The knock out will continue till the 4 top scoring teams make it to the semi-finals.
• Players must be present in the tournament area with the whole team at the schedule notified to them.
• Players are advised to be present 15 minutes before the schedule to avoid any unpredictable delay. If the team is not present on schedule, they shall be disqualified.
• The players have the freedom to bring and use their own controlling device, that is, Mouse (USB only) & Headphone.
• If players are willing to bring their own controllers, be sure it doesn't need any extra driver on Windows XP and also we take no responsibility for your stuffs.
• All players must have their clan tags before their name, without < or >.
• The team should be of at least 3 members and no change of players in the team is allowed after they have played their first match.
QUALIFICATION & QUARTERS ROUND: • There will be 12 rounds for each team, first 6 as a terrorist and second 6 as a counter terrorists and the team who FIRST wins 7 rounds will be declared the winner for that particular level.
• The starting money will be $800.
• In case of a tie or draw match, 3 extra rounds will be played continuously, the team winning 2 rounds will win that match.
• If both the teams are not satisfied while choosing a particular map from the available maps then a toss will be done to choose the maps.
SEMI-FINAL:
• The number of rounds will be 16 and one map will be played (Any one map will be selected by the winning team of the knife round).
• 8+8 rounds each as Counter Terrorist and Terrorist and the team who FIRST wins 9 rounds will be declared the winner for that particular level and advances to the finals.
• In case of a tie or draw match, 5 extra rounds will be played continuously, the team winning 3 rounds, will win that match.
FINALS:
• The number of rounds will be 24 and two maps will be played (maps will be selected by the teams).
• A surprise map can be used to conduct the finals.
• Only knife rounds or only sniper rounds can also be conducted in those 24 rounds and participants will be informed before the play starts.
• 12+12 rounds each as Counter Terrorist and Terrorist.
• The starting money will be $800.
• In case of a tie or draw match, 10 extra rounds will be played continuously, the team winning 6 rounds, will win that match.
If disconnection occurs during a match-
• If all players cannot play due to an unintended, unforeseen accident such as server stoppage, round will be restarted.
• In case of intentional disconnection, the referee may decide to end the match with the offending team losing.
• Protests can only be filed by the team leader - if any player does not agree to any rules, he/she can express his/her protest to referee before the completion of match. Any intentional refusal to connect to the match server can result in default loss for that team with the decision to be made at the sole discretion of the referee.
• If a rematch is decided by the head referee, the team that does not follow this decision will be subjected to disqualification.
• If one of the players is disconnected or crashed, he must come back in to the game as soon as possible and the round will be restarted.
• If half of the rounds of the match are already over, then only next half rounds will be restarted, else the whole match will be restarted.
• No compensation will be given for the money earned in game and lost due to interruption of the match.

DO'S AND DONT'S:
• Use of Tactical Shield is strictly forbidden.
• Following guns are not allowed.
1. Bull pup.
2. Krieg 550 Commando.
3. Krieg 552Commando.
4. Semi-Automatic sniper.
5. Scroll Walking is prohibited.
6. Using Bugs and Exploits are strictly prohibited.
7. If any player found intentionally delaying the knife rounds, the opponent team will be benefited.
"THE DECISION OF TOURNAMENT OFFICIALS, WILL BE THE FINAL DECISION"
