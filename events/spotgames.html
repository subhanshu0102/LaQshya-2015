A Min Game

INTRODUCTION

This is basically a gaming event where the participants will have only one minute to perform any tasks. The general games are:-
• Lightning candles in one minute.
• Making pyramid through disposals.

SPECIFICATION

• Individual Participant

RULES & REGULATIONS

General rules:
• The no. of qualifying participant for each round will be declared on the spot.
• Re-registration will be allowed.
Rules for lightning candle: • Only one match stick will provided to light candles in one minute.
• Winner will be judged by his/her no. of candles lightened as compared to others.
Rules for pyramid making:
• Pick only one disposal at a time.
• Pyramid should not scatter.
